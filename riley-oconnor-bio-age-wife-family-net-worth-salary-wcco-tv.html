Riley O’Connor Biography

Riley O’Connor is a seasoned American meteorologist currently working at WCCO since November 2019. You can always find his forecast on WCCO This Morning from 4:30 -7:00 a.m., as well as in the WCCO Mid-Morning show from 9:00 -10:00 a.a.

Riley was born and brought up in Evansville, Indiana, North America. He then attended Bosse High School for his high school education. After successfully completing high school, he joined Purdue University for an undergraduate degree. Later on, Riley pursued a meteorology degree from Mississippi State University.

O’Connor’s first job as a meteorologist was at KTIV-TV in Sioux City where he worked for three years. As a meteorologist, he has also worked in Portland, Oregon for three years preceding his role in KCCI.

Prior to joining WCCO, Riley was working at KCCI in Des Moines as a morning meteorologist.
