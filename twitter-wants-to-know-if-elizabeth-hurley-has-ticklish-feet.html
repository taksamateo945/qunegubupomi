Twitter Wants to Know if Elizabeth Hurley Has Ticklish Feet

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 7, 2024

Due to her level of fame, model/actress, Elizabeth Hurley, is probably used to being bombarded with questions from the press. However, thanks to social media, Hurley’s fans now have access to her in ways that did not exist before.  With the rise of the popular social networking website, Twitter, many of Hurley’s fans have used the platform as an attempt to get answers to some of their most burning questions.  However, instead of asking about her personal and romantic life, these fans all seem to want to know the same thing.  How ticklish are Elizabeth Hurley’s feet?  While this question may seem strange and useless, some of Hurley’s most dedicated fans will not rest until they receive an answer.  Here are three tweets from fans who want to know whether or not Elizabeth Hurley has ticklish feet.

 3.

@ElizabethHurley hi elizabeth could u tell me if u have ticklish feet plz

— carl castro (@carlcastro22) January 23, 2011

With hundreds of thousands of Twitter followers, Elizabeth Hurley is probably no stranger to weird questions like this, but she never seems to answer any questions regarding the sensitivity of her feet.  However, regardless of her silence on the topic, fans continue to ask.

2.

https://twitter.com/karlymhuggins/status/84615662756040704

Instead of posing a simple yes or no question like the Twitter user before her, @karlymhuggins decide s to ask Hurley to rate how ticklish her feet are on a scale of 0 to 10.  Although it is unclear why Huggins needs this information, Hurley’s lack of response proves that she didn’t see it as a pressing matter.

1.

https://twitter.com/ColeScotland/status/561320043448127488

Apparently, many of Elizabeth Hurley’s fans are curious about how ticklish her feet are.  Unfortunately for this user, mentioning that she needed the information for her dissertation wasn’t enough to get Hurley to respond to her tweet. Also, referring to Elizabeth Hurley as “Liz” doesn’t seemed to have raised her chances either. All we can do is hope that @ColeScotland’s grade didn’t depend on an answer.
