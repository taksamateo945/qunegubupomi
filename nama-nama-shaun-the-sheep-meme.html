What Happened to Meme Sheep?

Meme Sheep, a Youtube channel, which focused on the meme niche, was first terminated in March 2023. According to a source, the channel was deleted because of copyright issues. The channel got more than 2 copyright strikes which led to the YouTube channel’s deletion including all the contents.

This YouTube channel was joined in 2019 by an American person, who daily uploads the compilations of the memes. Although the channel’s strikes were rescinded in April, later in May the channel broke the rules of YouTube so the channel was once again deleted.
