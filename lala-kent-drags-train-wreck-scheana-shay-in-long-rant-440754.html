Lala Kent Drags Train Wreck Scheana Shay In Long Rant!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

It’s pretty clear at this point that Lala Kent and Scheana Shay have some major drama! The first took to her IG Stories to clap back at her Vanderpump Rules co-star Scheana Shay, calling her out for supposedly ‘making everything about her’ and more!

This comes after Scheana accused Lala of putting fame before their friendship which is why the latter wants people to know her side of the story as well!

And she held nothing back, going as far as to call her relationships a ‘trainwreck‘ and claim that she has a way of making everything all about her.

‘Anybody wondering my thoughts on @scheana ‘dragging’ me. 1. I’m not trippin too hard. In her mind that’s how it happened. She would rather focus on me ‘not being there for her’ than the fact her man said he was working, turned his phone off and went golfing that day. Weird how that was just glazed over. This is why her relationships are always a trainwreck that make epic TV. She makes reality TV gold,’ Lala wrote.

She went on: ‘2. She claims I chose celebrity over her. Girl, weren’t you just capitalizing off banging John Mayer 100 years ago? 3. She has a way of making everything about her. I’ve accepted that about her and found the endearing parts of it. 4. She has an idea of how everyone should react in certain situations. We usually always fail and she is left disappointed. This is why she has a new best friend every day. We can’t keep up.’

Smh.’

This is, of course, an answer to Scheana Shay’s claims that Lala Kent ignored her desperate request to come be with her after suffering a miscarriage during their vacation together.
