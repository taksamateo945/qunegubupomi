Justin Timberlake Chokes Up Talking to Young Fan at Brooklyn Show: Vid

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Tearin’ up our hearts! Justin Timberlake shared an emotional moment with a young fan during his show at Brooklyn’s Barclays Center on Sunday, Dec. 14. The interaction — which was filmed and posted to YouTube by a fellow concertgoer — brought both the 10-year-old boy and the 33-year-old pop superstar to near tears, as fans including Taylor Swift and Beyonce looked on.

As seen in the video, Timberlake took a break during the concert to acknowledge the white-suit-wearing fan, who handed the “Mirrors” singer a box containing a bow tie. “For me? How old are you, man?” Timberlake — whose wife, Jessica Biel, is pregnant with the couple’s first child — asked the kid.

Repeating the fan’s answer for the entire crowd, he said, “You’re 10, you’ve been watching me since you were 2.” As Timberlake unwrapped the gift, the boy started to cry, prompting the dad-to-be to plead with him from the stage, “Come on, man. Come on, little man!”
