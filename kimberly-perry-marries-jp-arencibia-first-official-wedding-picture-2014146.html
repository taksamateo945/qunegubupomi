Kimberly Perry Marries J.P. Arencibia: First Official Wedding Pictures

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Pretty Perry! As previously reported, Kimberly Perry married Texas Rangers catcher J.P. Arencibia in Greeneville, Tenn., on Thursday, June 12. And now, Us Weekly can exclusively reveal the first official wedding photo of the new bride and groom!

The Band Perry singer, 30, and Arencibia, 28, tied the knot at the First Presbyterian Church. They were feted by close family and friends—including country singer Carrie Underwood and her husband Mike Fisher.

Perry looked beautiful in a Reem Acra gown and held a large bouquet of white flowers as she walked down the aisle. Brothers Reid and Neil Perry were her best men, while Arencibia’s young nieces were his groomswomen. Nashville stars Lennon and Maisy Stella and Mac Powell also performed.
